Soy Chicken

Description

This recipe details a soy chicken recipe, one of my personal favourites as it is easy to make and reheat for my weekly meal preps

Ingredients:

Raw Materials/Ingredients

  • 1.25 - 1.5 kg/ 2.5 - 3 lb skinless boneless chicken thighs OR breast
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Honey Soy Marinade Sauce:

  • 125 ml soy sauce
  • 225 g honey
  • 85 ml cider vinegar
  • 65 ml Chinese cooking wine or dry sherry
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1.5 tbsp garlic

Serving Sauce:

  • 85 ml water
  • 1.5 tsp cornflour/cornstarch

Steps

Honey Soy Marinade Sauce:

  1. Place ingredients in a jug and mix well until honey is completely dissolved.
  2. Reserve 1/4 cup for Basting, refrigerate.
  3. Reserve 2/3 cup for Serving Sauce, refrigerate.
  4. Marinade Chicken: Pour remaining marinade over chicken in a container. Coat chicken thoroughly. Cover, refrigerate 24 to 48 hours (minimum 3 hrs).

Cook:

  1. Brush BBQ plate or grills with oil and heat to medium high. Or heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.
  2. THIGHS: Place chicken on BBQ. Cook the first side for 2 minutes, then flip and dab with reserved Basting Sauce. Cook for 2 minutes, flip and baste. Then flip/baste every 1 minute for total cook time of ~ 10 minutes or until a thermometer reads 75째C/165째F.
  3. BREAST: Cook first side 2 minutes, flip, baste. Cook 2 minutes, then flip and baste every 45 sec or so for total cook time of ~8 minutes (internal temp 66째C / 151째F).
  4. REST: Transfer to serving plate, cover loosely with foil and rest for 5 minutes.
  5. SERVE chicken garnished with sesame seeds and green onion, with sauce on the side so people can help themselves.
